01 // THE AESTHETIC DOCTRINE

This production is built on the "High-Contrast Shadow" principle. We are moving away from flat, bright lighting. We want deep blacks, sharp highlights on the skin, and a sense of cold, corporate dominance. You are a high-level executive in a glass-and-steel office. The power is yours; the viewer is the subordinate.

02 // LIGHTING & CAMERA SPECS
Key Lighting: 45-degree Rembrandt lighting. High shadows on the non-lit side of the face.
Rim Light: Cool blue or stark white backlight to separate silhouette from the dark background.
Framing: Low-angle shots to increase perceived status. 8K resolution mandatory.
Dynamic Range: Focus on 10-bit color. Black levels must be pure #000.
03 // WARDROBE REQUIREMENT

Strict adherence to the Obsidian Palette. No bright colors or distracting patterns.
